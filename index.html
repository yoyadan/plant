I am a Master Candidate of Architecture and Urban Planning at the School of Civil Engineering in Shandong University. I like to think about the relationship between architecture and society. For me, the past, present and future can be reflected in the evolution of architecture throughout its life, and architecture can also witness and promote the development of society.
